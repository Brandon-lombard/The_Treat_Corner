Learn about us

Bringing sweet joy to every bite

Welcome to The Treat Corner, a home bakery where every bite is made with care, creativity, and a love for sweet treats. What started as a passion for baking in our home kitchen quickly grew into something special — a place where we could share our favourite desserts with family, friends, and our community. At The Treat Corner, we focus on creating delicious, freshly made treats that bring a little extra joy to every occasion. Our menu features a variety of homemade favourites, including Malva pudding cups, milk tart cups, cake pops, chocolate-dunked Oreos, brownies, and rice krispy treat squares. Each treat is carefully prepared using quality ingredients to ensure every bite is just as satisfying as the last. Because we are a home bakery, every order receives personal attention. We take pride in small-batch baking, which allows us to maintain the quality and flavour that our customers love. Whether you’re celebrating a special occasion, planning a gathering, or simply craving something sweet, The Treat Corner is here to make your day a little sweeter. Thank you for supporting our small business and being part of our baking journey.
